Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Clients may cancel services by submitting a written request to NAJM Engineering Ltd.
  • The terms of cancellation and any associated costs depend on the stage of the project and the terms outlined in the service agreement.
  • Refunds may be considered only under specific conditions, such as if work has not yet begun or if cancellation occurs within an agreed cancellation window.
  • No refunds will be issued for services that are already rendered or partially completed.
  • Any approved refunds may be subject to administrative or processing fees.
  • This policy is designed to ensure fairness to both parties and protect the integrity of project timelines and resource allocations.
