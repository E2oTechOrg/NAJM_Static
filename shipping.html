Shipping / Delivery Policy

  • NAJM Engineering Ltd does not provide physical products.
  • All deliverables—such as reports, engineering drawings, and technical documentation—are delivered digitally via email or cloud-based platforms.
  • In certain situations, physical submissions can be arranged based on contractual agreements or regulatory requirements.
  • Delivery timelines are established at the beginning of each project and depend on scope, regulatory approvals, and third-party dependencies.
  • We make every effort to meet agreed delivery schedules and will promptly notify clients of any potential delays.
