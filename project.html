Services

Land Development

Our comprehensive land development services encompass site planning, zoning analysis, and infrastructure design to support residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We navigate municipal approvals and deliver efficient, code-compliant solutions tailored to each property.

Greenhouse Developments

We design specialized infrastructure to support large-scale greenhouse facilities, including access roads, water and energy distribution, and site grading, ensuring optimal growing conditions and regulatory compliance.

Commercial & Industrial Engineering

We offer tailored civil engineering solutions for commercial and industrial sites, including traffic access, utility coordination, stormwater management, and site servicing to support long-term business growth.

Municipal Engineering

We deliver detailed engineering designs for roads, watermains, and sewers. Our municipal services support community development and include planning, approvals, and contract administration.

Stormwater Management

Our stormwater systems mitigate flooding and improve water quality. We prepare detailed drainage plans, storm sewer designs, and modeling reports to meet municipal and environmental standards.

Sanitary Sewer Analysis

We perform capacity analysis and modeling of sanitary sewer systems to evaluate infrastructure needs, assess development impacts, and ensure compliance with municipal guidelines.

Water Demand Analysis

Our engineers assess water supply requirements for new developments and existing infrastructure using advanced modeling to ensure efficient and sustainable distribution systems.

Functional Servicing Studies

We prepare Functional Servicing Reports (FSRs) that evaluate servicing options for water, wastewater, and stormwater in support of development applications and planning approvals.

Project Management

From concept to completion, our project managers coordinate engineering design, municipal approvals, tendering, and construction oversight, ensuring budget control and on-time delivery.

Surveying & Construction Inspection

Our team provides topographic and legal surveying, construction layout, and quality assurance inspections to ensure projects are built according to approved design and specifications.

Tender Preparation & Evaluation

We prepare comprehensive tender packages including drawings, specifications, and contract documents. Our team evaluates contractor bids to ensure competitive pricing and contractor capability.

Drainage & Floodplain Modeling

We conduct detailed drainage and floodplain modeling using advanced hydrologic and hydraulic software to support flood risk assessments and regulatory approvals.
